Welcome to my portfolio! I'm Aritra Mitra, a XIIth grade student at Mallya Aditi International School, following the IGCSE international curriculum. Over the past four years, I have immersed myself in the world of art and design, exploring various creative avenues. This website showcases my journey, with a focus on human portraiture, photography of India's national parks, and influences from cinema and popular culture. Join me as I unveil my artistic endeavors and passion!

About me: 

As an aspiring artist, I've dedicated myself to art and design, starting from my O level education in the IGCSE curriculum. Captivated by human portraiture, I strive to capture unique personalities and emotions, celebrating the human spirit. 

Beyond portraits, I find joy in photographing India's national parks, showcasing the intricate flora and fauna. Through my lens, I hope to raise awareness and inspire conservation. 

An avid cinema enthusiast, I draw from iconic characters and scenes, infusing diverse influences into my art.
